Hey dollface
Dancefloor dreaming? Us too.

Here, sweet pea, are your stages. You'll find them rippling with utterly brilliant artists, performers and musicians gleaned from across the planet.

From the huge jaw-dropping brilliance you will witness on our Main Stage, to the grassroots glimmers under the eaves in the Folk Barn. The arts and icons on The Atrium, the sweaty late-night disco jams in The Hustle, the free-flow wildness of The Carousel and the left-field, other-worldly wonders at The Love Hotel.

And of course, the writhing, fancy-dressed, mass euphoria you can only find in The Valley...

We really do have all bases covered. Explore your Wilderness future.

Your welcome.
